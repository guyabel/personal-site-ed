Robotic Devices for Future Nuclear Plant?

Robotic Devices for Future Nuclear Plant?

E. Abel

Abstract

Robots can present cost effective alternatives to man and conventional manipulators in nuclear facilities. The historical background to the development of servomanipulators

and robots is reviewed, the types of application areas suited to robots are discussed, and recommendations are made for further developments. In the light of this review, a programme considering the use of robotic and teleoperated devices in nuclear environments commenced in August 1984 at AERE Harwell.
